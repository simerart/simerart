Welcome to Me

Simera Robertson

Graphic Designer| Photographer

I'm an unconventional graphic designer with hippie roots living in my converted school bus. My work includes Graphic Design, concert photography and Super8 film capturing raw musical energy on the road. Friends compare my quirky personality to Phoebe from Friends. I seek collaborations with like minded creatives where I can freely ideate, apply my design skills. I thrive in environments valuing both excellence and authentic self-expression.

I'm available for hire as a freelance creative. I'm actively seeking challenging new projects that push creative boundaries.
